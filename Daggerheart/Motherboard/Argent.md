Argent, the jewel of the Veil, stands as a marvel of engineering, both of the Fallen and the newer civilization which has come to live in its shell. It rises above the Silver Lake, constructed entirely on artificial platforms,  which it inherited in large part. It defends itself against foreign threats by use of the Barrier, the great wall which its residents built more than 200 years ago, guided by the ever-watchful Priv.

History:
- Argent was built on the ruins of a large city. 
- Sera, the Architect, achieved its greatest feat (constructing The Barrier), by uniting smaller factions towards a common cause: building a settlement that can keep the Wastes out and prosperity in. 
- Sera was the first "Priv", the person appointed to oversee the wellbeing of the city. She promised that when the Barrier was complete, she'd resign her role and let another be elected Priv, and she kept her promise.
- This happened 200 years ago.
- Since then, Argent has remained a stable democracy, but has also become a police state, driven by a constant desire for stability above all else.
- Argent is often attacked, raided, scouted or even sieged by the carrowcraft walkaway, but their walls have kept them safe so far.

Common opinions of citizens of Argent:
- Argent is exceptional, we are civilized while others are savage
- Argent is dominant, we are wealthy while others are desperate
- Argent is advanced, we use tech, and especially remnants, while others reject it

Resources:
- Argent inherited well-working hydroponics, like every walled city, allowing them to have reliable crops year-round.
- Argent has a population of 500.000 people,  the largest of all cities, allowing for labour and trade and wealth accumulation.
- Argent has wealth, meaning they can get whatever they can't make by dealing with others
- Argent has a strong City Guard, a police force and standing army which defends against raiders (such as those from the Walkaway)
- Argent is bracketed by mountains on three sides, and the Barrier on the fourth, making it easy to  defend
- Argent has water, being fed by the river Signal, which diverts itself from its path towards the Sea to pool into the Silver Lake.
- Argent has skilled engineers and technomancers, who travel from across the Vale to find a better, more stable life here.
