I could then also start coaching people for extra, high-enjoyment income. But I do know what a good decision for my career looks like on any given day. And so, it feels possible to be hopeful and excited rather than to dread the future, to be afraid of what might come or what might not work. So why don't I feel hopeful? Why don't I spring out of bed thinking "let's see what awesome things we can do today to live an authentic life, true to ourselves, our values, our passions and loves, how best to take care of ourselves today and be a good person in the world"? How can I feel more content and happier and enthused? Sometimes enthusiasm feels childish. I don't know how I've become cynical and how I can heal from that and be earnestly enthusiastic again.
